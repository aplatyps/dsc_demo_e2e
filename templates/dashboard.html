Job Recruitment Fraud Dataset

EMSCAD contains 17,014 legitimate and 866 fraudulent job ads (17,880 in total) published between 2012 to 2014.
Available at Employment Scam Aegean Dataset http://emscad.samos.aegean.gr/

Some insights to the original dataset:

  1. Dataset is highly unbalanced
  2. Dataset contains blank entries (NaN values) due to factors such as non disclosure of information
  3. Dataset contains duplicates, possibly due to identical job advertisement posted with slightly different variables such as different locations or salary
  4. Dataset may contain some misclassified samples due to human error from manually annotated samples by specialized Workable employees

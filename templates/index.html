Employment Fraud


Background

Most hiring posts are published online that aim to make hiring process more immediate, accurate and cost-efficient. However, online exposure of such traditional business procedures has introduced scam problems that may lead to serious loss both for job applicants and employers. Employment fraud has been rampant especially during difficult times when many are vulnerable and in dire need of jobs.


Problem Statement

Employment fraud is a issue worth investigating as it can inflict loss of privacy, economic damage or harm the reputation of all stakeholders. Many job posting websites do not regulate the posts uploaded to their websites or verify employers which enables spam posting of fraudulent job post. Even when countermeasures exist, they are usually ad-hoc and their practical value is often questionable. Not to mention today, job frauds has become more resourceful and difficult to distinguish their legitimacy.


How Can We Fight Fraudulent Posts?


Automated Detection with Deep Learning

One way to deal tackle job fraud is to create a model that automatically detects and take down fraudulent job postings. A simple classification task can be done with deep learning, which are data-driven and good at handling high-dimensional data.
